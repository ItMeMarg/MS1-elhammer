Ylva/Ulf Elhammer is a non-binary queer Seattle-based tattoo apprentice. In early 2018, they began apprenticing under Jude Le Tronik, founder and owner of Lilith Tattoo .

Prior to tattooing, Elhammer earned a degree in Art and Design from the University of Michigan. You can find examples of their illustrative as well as tattoo projects in their portfolio.

Elhammer currently works exclusively out of Lilith Tattoo located in Seattle, USA. Their rate is $75 per hour. While their books are currently closed for tattooing, they are welcoming illustrative pieces. Please reach out.

They currently live in Ballard with their dog, Spaghetti.
